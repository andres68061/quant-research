Area	Risk	Severity	What's happening
Survivorship bias	YES	CRITICAL	backfill_all.py fetches today's S&P 500 list and backtests on it for 10 years. Companies that were removed (Lehman, GE at times, acquired firms) are excluded. Historical constituents code exists (core/data/sp500_constituents.py) but is not wired into the pipeline.
Macro lookahead	YES	CRITICAL	load_default_macro() uses FRED series dates as-is with forward-fill. CPI for January appears on Jan 1 in the data, but it's actually released mid-February. Same for unemployment, fed funds. Daily yields (DGS10, T10Y2Y) are fine — minimal lag.
Same-close trading	PARTIAL	MEDIUM	calculate_portfolio_returns computes signals from close prices, then assumes you trade at the same close — physically impossible. Standard fix: 1-day delay between signal and execution.
Market cap historical accuracy	YES	MEDIUM	historical_market_caps.parquet = today's shares outstanding x historical adjusted prices. Buybacks/dilution over time are ignored, biasing size rankings for buyback-heavy (large, quality) stocks.
Momentum formula	NO (bug)	LOW	momentum_excluding_recent uses arithmetic subtraction cum_12m - cum_1m instead of geometric (1+cum_12m)/(1+cum_1m) - 1. Not lookahead, but produces slightly different rankings.
Fundamentals	NO	Safe	60-day as-of lag is correctly applied via apply_asof_lag.
FF5	PARTIAL	LOW	No publication lag, but only used for attribution (not signals) currently.
ML features	NO	Safe	Properly .shift(1) lagged.
Data snooping	NO	LOW	All defaults are standard academic/industry values.
